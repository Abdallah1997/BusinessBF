# PRODUCT.md — BusinessBF

## Product Purpose
BusinessBF is an all-in-one operating system for online resellers: inventory with
real cost of goods, listings tracked across every marketplace, profit per sale,
tax-ready bookkeeping, and AI that does the tedious parts (receipt reading, bank
transaction sorting, email order import, listing writing). One tool replacing two
subscriptions (SellerLedger for books, TrackNList for crosslisting).

## Users
Solo and small-team resellers flipping thrift, vintage, sneakers and collectibles
on eBay, Poshmark, Mercari, Depop and Facebook Marketplace. They work from phones
and laptops between sourcing runs and post-office trips. Spreadsheet-tired, not
accountants; they want to trust the numbers without learning bookkeeping. The
owner runs his own reselling business on it and intends to sell it as SaaS.

product

## Brand & Tone
Confident, plain-spoken, working-class professional: the well-lit stockroom, not
the corporate boardroom. Copy is direct and concrete ("Know your profit on every
flip"), never hype ("Elevate your selling journey" is banned). Numbers are the
hero; the UI frames them quietly. Trust signals matter everywhere money appears:
say plainly what is stored, what is not, and what AI will and will not do without
confirmation.

## Strategic Principles
- AI suggests, the user confirms. Nothing books itself.
- Never store bank credentials or full account numbers; say so in the UI.
- The user's data is exportable at all times. No lock-in.
- Inventory, listings and books share one source of truth; numbers must agree.
- Works without any AI key configured; AI is an accelerant, not a dependency.

## Anti-references
- QuickBooks: powerful but intimidating; we are the opposite of a chart-of-accounts maze.
- Crypto-dashboard aesthetics: neon on black, glow, fake urgency.
- Generic AI-SaaS landing pages: purple gradients, sparkle-everything, invented metrics.
